My Poster Designs

This is me

Himel.

A son of Dhaka.
A storyteller of shadows.
A traveler of frames.

A believer that every moment can be cinematic if you dare to feel it deeply enough.

Born in 2002, raised by the restless heartbeat of Dhaka — a city I don’t just live in, but belong to. Dhaka isn’t my address; it’s my first love, my longest poem.

I run my own production house, HM Production, where I work as director and story writer — shaping imagination into visual reality.

Every frame I create carries emotion — sometimes loud like a festival, sometimes quiet like a lonely streetlight.

I design posters, create films, capture photographs, and build digital spaces — blending technology and art into one vision.

My portfolio isn’t just a gallery.
It’s a film.

A film about a boy from Dhaka
who learned to see beauty
where others saw ordinary life.
